The Best Movies According to Raymond

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Last Page
Balmiki, an arrogant non-conformist writer of fiction. Explores the very basic concept of debt and the idea of freedom attached to the word.

Dynasties
Follow the true stories of five of the world's most celebrated, yet endangered animals; penguins, chimpanzees, lions, painted wolves and tigers. Each in a heroic struggle against rivals and against the forces of nature.

Avatar: The Last Airbender
In a war-torn world of elemental magic, a young boy reawakens to undertake a dangerous mystic quest to fulfill his destiny as the Avatar, and bring peace to the world.
